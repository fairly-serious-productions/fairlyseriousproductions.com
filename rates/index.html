Hourly Consultation

We bill $50 per hour on creative consulting, which includes:

  • Ideation and story-beat development
  • Editing and refinement of written materials
  • Game/puzzle/mechanic design advice
  • Directorial and dramaturgical support
  • Similar theatrical, authorial, or editorial services

We encourage potential clients to meet with us for a free consultation to discuss your project and see if our studio suits your needs and vision.

Co-Authorship

For significant adaptations, new builds, or integration of built-for-show software, we prefer to work as co-authors rather than consultants. Story, mechanics, pacing, and tools all interlock; you'll get better results with us in the room as collaborators, able to design, iterate, and stand behind the whole experience.

As co-authors, we retain joint authorial credit and charge 8% of Gross Box Office per performance, less agreed taxes and processing fees.

For buyouts or unticketed events, the same 8% applies to the show fee. Night-of services (performing, facilitation, and technical operations) can be included at an additional rate.
